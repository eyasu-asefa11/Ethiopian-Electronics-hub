HIGH

🔍 Advanced Search & Filters

Smart search with filters for price range, brand, condition, location, availability, and customer ratings. Auto-suggestions and spelling correction.

HIGH

⭐ Customer Reviews & Ratings

Star ratings, detailed reviews, photo reviews, review verification, and shop owner responses. Build trust and credibility.

HIGH

💬 Real-Time Chat System

Live chat between customers and shop owners, file sharing, chat history, and mobile notifications. Instant communication.

HIGH

🛒 Shopping Cart & Checkout

Add to cart, wishlist, multiple payment methods (Telebirr, CBE Birr, bank transfer), order tracking, and email confirmations.

HIGH

📱 Customer Dashboard

Order history, saved addresses, favorite shops, price alerts, and personalized recommendations based on browsing history.

HIGH

📸 Product Photo Gallery

Multiple high-quality photos, 360° views, zoom functionality, and customer-uploaded photos showing actual usage.

MEDIUM

MEDIUM

Google Maps integration, shop locations, distance calculation, route planning, and nearby shop suggestions.
