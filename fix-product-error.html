🔧 Fix Product Addition Error

❌ Problem: Product addition fails because there are no registered shops in the system.
✅ Solution: Register a shop first, then you can add products to that shop.

📋 Step-by-Step Fix:

  1. Register a Shop: Click "Register New Shop" button below
  2. Fill Shop Details: Complete shop registration form with shop name, address, etc.
  3. Upload Shop Images: Add shop logo and images
  4. Submit Registration: Complete shop registration
  5. Add Products: After shop is registered, you can add products
  6. Test Product Addition: Products will now save successfully
